Digital Card QR + vCard Flow

Use this flow when sharing Adab contact details at events, client meetings, and property inspections.

  1. Share /brand/downloads/digital/digital-card.html as the public digital card.
  2. Use the button inside the digital card to download adab-contact.vcf.
  3. For print materials, scan the QR below. It points to the digital card URL.
  4. After scan, user taps Add to contacts (.vcf).
